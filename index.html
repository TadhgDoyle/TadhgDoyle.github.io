Background

Born on 28th February 2012 in Portiuncula University Hospital, Ballinasloe, Ireland. Tadhg has been raised in 'Carrownure Lower', a townland near the small village of Lecarrow in South Roscommon. Obsessed with football since he could walk, Tadhg's stated aim in life, since he could express himself, is to to become a professional footballer. This remains his vision for himself and his ultimate goal. During his early years Tadhg played with the local 'St. John's Athletic' team. Following the relaxation of covid restrictions, it was decided to give Tadhg the opportunity to play his football in Dublin within the DDSL league.

Overview

Tadhg is an incredibly skilled, aware, technical midfielder. Somewhat in the vein of players such as Scholes, Iniesta, Verrati and Modric. Genuinely and naturally two footed. His engine is well attested, measured and acknowledged, making him a true box to box player. Tadhg's preferred role is that of a '6', securing the central areas of the pitch while ensuring efficient transition between the thirds. Extremely versatile , Tadhg can perform and excel in any midfield role, play wing back on either side, or even play a false 9 role.

Attributes

Tadhg's main attributes are his:

  • Unquestionable commitment and self belief. Applied to every training session, match, and opportunity to better himself. Driven to win, losing an unwanted but sometimes necessary opportunity to learn.
  • Tadhg likes to lead by example and be vocal in a positive manner. A player's player.
  • Tadhg's technical skills are certainly the equal of any other '2012' player in the DDSL league. A gifted 'natural' footballer.
  • Never the biggest player on the pitch (nor the smallest), Tadhg uses his speed, balance, agility, endurance, tactical nous, his physical and mental toughness to best his opponents. Brave and committed in the tackle.
  • Football is his passion. He is driven to push his limits, to learn, apply and succeed.
